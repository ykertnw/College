Agridera Fresh Market Tomato Hybrids

Agridera aims to be one of the world's leading companies in the breeding, production, and marketing of tomato seeds. We invest intensively in research and development to provide solutions to growers and consumers.

Agridera Tomato breeding programs are focusing on three main segments: Greenhouse Indeterminate Tomatoes, Open-field Indeterminate Tomatoes, and Bush Type Open-field Tomatoes.
